  - "Manage DNS"

Click on it for levqor.ai domain.


STEP 4: ADD CNAME RECORD
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Click "Add Record" or "Add New Record"

Fill in:
  Type:  CNAME
  Host:  api
  Value: levqor-backend.replit.app
  TTL:   Automatic (or 3600)

Click "Save" or "Add Record"
