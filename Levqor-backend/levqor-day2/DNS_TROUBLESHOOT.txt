COMMON PROBLEMS TO FIX:
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

❌ Multiple A records for "api"
   → DELETE all extra A records, keep only one

❌ AAAA record for "api" 
   → DELETE the AAAA record (IPv6)

❌ Orange cloud on A record
   → Click to turn GRAY (DNS only)

❌ CNAME record for "api"
   → DELETE it (use A record instead)


STEPS TO FIX:

2. Check for "api" records - you should see EXACTLY:
   - 1 A record (34.111.179.208, proxy OFF)
   - 1 TXT record (replit-verify=...)
