# Levqor Support FAQ

## What is Levqor?
Levqor is an AI automation platform that builds production-ready workflows for small businesses. We offer both Done-For-You (DFY) automation builds and subscription-based automation services.

## What services do you offer?

### Done-For-You (DFY) Services
- **DFY Starter (£99):** 1 workflow, 48-hour delivery, 7 days support
- **DFY Professional (£249):** 3 workflows, 3-4 day delivery, 30 days support, monitoring dashboard
- **DFY Enterprise (£599):** 7 workflows, advanced logic, self-healing automations, priority support

### Subscription Plans
- **Growth (£29/month):** Basic automation with 100 tasks/month
- **Business (£99/month):** Advanced automation with 1,000 tasks/month
- **Enterprise (£299/month):** Unlimited automation with dedicated support

## How long does delivery take?
- **DFY Starter:** 24-48 hours
- **DFY Professional:** 3-4 business days
- **DFY Enterprise:** 7 business days

## What is your refund policy?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for both DFY and subscription services. If you're not satisfied, contact support@levqor.ai.

## How do I get started?
1. Choose your plan at www.levqor.ai/pricing
2. Complete secure checkout via Stripe
3. Fill out the intake form (5 minutes)
4. We schedule a kickoff call
5. We build, test, and deliver your automation

## What tools can you integrate?
We work with most popular business tools including:
- CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive)
- Email (Gmail, Outlook, Mailchimp)
- Spreadsheets (Google Sheets, Airtable)
- Communication (Slack, Telegram, WhatsApp)
- Payment (Stripe, PayPal)
- And 500+ more via API connections

## Do you handle high-risk data?
No. We prohibit medical, legal, financial, and safety-critical data processing for compliance reasons. See our Terms of Service for details.

## How do I contact support?
- Email: support@levqor.ai
- Sales: sales@levqor.ai
- Privacy: privacy@levqor.ai
- Emergency (SEV1): See /emergency page

## Can I upgrade my plan?
Yes! You can upgrade from DFY to subscriptions or move to a higher tier anytime. Contact sales@levqor.ai for assistance.

## What happens after delivery?
- You receive full documentation and video walkthrough
- Support continues for 7-30 days (depending on tier)
- We monitor your automation for issues
- You can request revisions based on your tier

## Is my data secure?
Yes. We are GDPR/PECR compliant and use:
- End-to-end encryption
- SOC 2 Type II infrastructure (Vercel, Stripe)
- Regular security audits
- Strict data retention policies

## Can I cancel my subscription?
Yes, you can cancel anytime from your dashboard. Cancellations take effect at the end of your billing period.
