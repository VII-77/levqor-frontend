# Levqor Policies Summary

## Privacy & Data Protection

### GDPR/PECR Compliance
- Full compliance with UK GDPR and PECR regulations
- Data Processing Agreement available on request
- EU-UK data transfer safeguards in place
- Privacy policy at www.levqor.ai/privacy

### Data Collection
We collect:
- Account information (name, email, company)
- Payment details (processed by Stripe)
- Usage data (workflow executions, API calls)
- Technical logs (IP addresses, browser info)

We DO NOT collect:
- Medical or health data
- Legal or court documents
- Financial account credentials
- Biometric or genetic data

### Data Retention
- Active accounts: Data retained while account active
- Inactive accounts: 90 days after last activity
- Billing records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Logs: 90 days
- Backups: 30 days

### Your Rights
- Right to access your data (DSAR requests)
- Right to deletion ("Right to be forgotten")
- Right to correction
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to restrict processing

**To exercise rights:** privacy@levqor.ai or use /data-requests page

## High-Risk Data Prohibition

For compliance and liability reasons, Levqor prohibits automation involving:

### Medical Data
- Patient health records
- Medical diagnoses or treatment plans
- Prescription or medication data
- Health insurance claims

### Legal Data
- Court filings or legal proceedings
- Client-attorney privileged communications
- Contracts with legal binding
- Immigration or visa applications

### Financial Data
- Banking account credentials
- Investment advice or stock trading
- Loan applications or credit decisions
- Tax return preparation

### Safety-Critical Systems
- Emergency services automation
- Life safety systems
- Industrial control systems
- Transportation safety systems

**Why?** These categories require specialized compliance (HIPAA, FCA, etc.) and carry significant liability risks.

**Alternative:** For enterprise-grade automation in these domains, contact sales@levqor.ai for custom compliance solutions.
