## 1. WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, analyze usage, and improve functionality.

## 2. HOW WE USE COOKIES

EchoPilot uses cookies and similar technologies to:
- Maintain your login session
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze how you use the Service
- Improve performance and user experience
- Ensure security

## 3. TYPES OF COOKIES WE USE

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies
These cookies are essential for the Service to function:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintain your login session | Session (deleted when browser closes) |
| `csrf_token` | Prevent cross-site request forgery attacks | Session |

**Legal Basis:** These cookies are necessary for contract performance (providing the Service). You cannot opt-out of strictly necessary cookies.

### 3.2 Functional Cookies
These cookies remember your preferences:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies
These cookies help us understand how you use the Service:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |

## 12. TECHNICAL DETAILS

### 12.1 Session vs. Persistent Cookies
- **Session Cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them

### 12.2 First-Party vs. Third-Party Cookies
- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by EchoPilot (echopilotai.replit.app)
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services (Stripe, analytics providers)

### 12.3 How to Delete Cookies
**Desktop Browsers:**
1. Open browser settings
2. Navigate to Privacy or Cookies section
3. Click "Clear browsing data" or "Delete cookies"
4. Select time range and confirm

**Mobile Browsers:**
1. Open browser app settings
2. Find Privacy or Site settings
3. Tap "Clear cookies" or "Clear site data"
