## FILMING VARIATIONS

### Style A: Talking Head
- Film yourself talking directly to camera
- Use simple background (office, home, clean space)
- Good for credibility and personal connection

### Style B: Screen Recording
- Record your screen showing the automation working
- Add voiceover explaining what's happening
- Good for showing proof and process

### Style C: Before/After Split
- Left side: you stressed, doing manual work
- Right side: you relaxed, automation running
- Good for visual impact

### Style D: Text-Only
- No face, just text overlays and B-roll
- Fast-paced, punchy copy
- Good if camera-shy
